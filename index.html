Share Your Jam!

JukeBox is a social platform for music rating. Find your friends using JukeBox, put in your favorite songs, and rate people's music. (See this week's favorite songs) We learn what you like and recommend songs to you from what you liked in the past.

Rate and Review

JukeBox allows you to discuss your favorite songs and albums in a forum. See if you agree with what others are saying and if you don't, leave a comment telling everyone why they are wrong.

Get Personalized Recomondations

With our music smarts, we recommend songs we know you'll like, while also finding new genres and artists that we think you should try :)
